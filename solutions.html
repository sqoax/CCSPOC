Core Solutions

Deployment support for the technology healthcare teams rely on every day.

Each solution is designed around consistency, clinical workflow, room readiness, and reliable execution inside active healthcare environments.

01

Patient Room Technology Deployment

SPOC supports the planning and installation of technology inside modern patient rooms, helping healthcare teams create consistent, reliable, and functional point-of-care environments.

What this includes

  • In-room displays and monitors
  • Wall-mounted workstations
  • Bedside technology
  • Patient engagement equipment
  • Device mounting and placement
  • Room-by-room standardization
Why it matters

Patient rooms need technology that is easy to access, cleanly installed, and aligned with clinical workflow.

02

Equipment & Device Installation

From mounts and carts to displays and specialty equipment, SPOC provides professional installation support for the hardware healthcare teams rely on every day.

What this includes

  • Mounts
  • Workstations
  • Carts
  • Displays
  • Specialty equipment
  • Accessories and device components
Why it matters

Clean, consistent installation reduces friction, improves usability, and helps technology perform reliably in live clinical environments.

03

Cable & Infrastructure Standardization

Messy, inconsistent installations create reliability problems and make support harder. SPOC helps healthcare systems standardize cabling, equipment placement, and room-level infrastructure across deployments.

What this includes

  • Cable routing and organization
  • Room-level technology standards
  • Equipment placement consistency
  • Cleanup of messy installs
  • Reliability improvements
  • Support-friendly configurations
Why it matters

Standardized infrastructure makes rooms easier to support, easier to maintain, and easier to scale across a healthcare system.

04

Large-Scale Hospital Rollouts

SPOC supports coordinated rollouts across rooms, departments, units, and facilities, helping healthcare systems execute complex technology deployments with consistency and minimal disruption.

What this includes

  • Multi-unit deployment
  • Multi-facility coordination
  • Project scheduling
  • Vendor coordination
  • Equipment staging
  • Installation sequencing
  • Go-live support
Why it matters

Large healthcare deployments require coordination, consistency, and teams that understand how to work inside active clinical environments.
