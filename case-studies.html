01 Featured Project

Hospital System Rollout

A coordinated point-of-care technology deployment across multiple departments within an active hospital environment.

Project Overview

A regional healthcare system needed to standardize point-of-care technology across several high-traffic clinical areas while minimizing disruption to daily operations. SPOC coordinated planning, equipment staging, installation sequencing, and go-live support.

Outcome

The deployment created a more consistent technology environment across clinical areas, improved room-to-room standardization, and gave internal teams a single accountable partner throughout the rollout.

8 Departments supported
140+ Rooms planned
1 Coordinated deployment team

Scope of Deployment

  • Multi-department rollout
  • Patient room technology updates
  • Workstation and display installation
  • Cable organization and room-level standardization
  • Coordination with IT, facilities, and clinical leadership

Technology Installed

  • Wall-mounted workstations
  • In-room displays
  • Mobile computer carts
  • Device mounts and accessories
  • Cable management components
02 Project Example

Patient Room Modernization

A patient room technology refresh designed to improve usability, consistency, and supportability across an occupied care environment.

Project Overview

An active hospital needed to modernize patient room technology without creating unnecessary disruption for clinicians or patients. SPOC supported equipment planning, room-by-room installation, testing, and final readiness checks.

Outcome

The project helped create cleaner, more usable patient room technology setups while supporting consistency across rooms and simplifying future maintenance.

60+ Patient rooms refreshed
Std. Room technology packages
Live Clinical environment coordination

Scope of Deployment

  • Patient room modernization
  • Device placement and mounting
  • Display and workstation installation
  • Infrastructure cleanup
  • Go-live readiness support

Technology Installed

  • Patient room displays
  • Bedside technology components
  • Wall mounts
  • Clinical workstations
  • Cable routing and organization
